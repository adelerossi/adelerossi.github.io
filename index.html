Magis, Optimization Customization Tool.
Digital Strategy for Magis Design Company. Learn more

Magis is the Italian Brand, partnership with major international designers and focused on design products for home, office and contract. Each Magis product is created to the highest possible quality, reflecting the remarkable skills and knowledge of his partners, ethical and poetic as well as aesthetic products and the exuberant creativity with technological research.
The project strategy is the optimization of the Customization Tool and redesign the website to establish costumer loyalty and build a strong relationship, satisfaction and emotional attachment through the creation of an immersive, virtual and interactive experience.

‘Las Plantas’, Editorial project realized for Marta Valpiana.

Learn more

ALOE is a magazine deals the artistic project of the Italian visual artist Marta Valpiana (Verona, 1993) and her project called “Neophyte”.

The artist analyzes the experience of plants in human life to understand her world, and consequently ours. For this reason each monograph has a precise date, which is the equinox of: Autumn (22 September 2019; 07:50 h); Spring (20 March 2019; 22:58 h) and the solstice of Winter ( 21 December 2019; 04:19 h) and Summer ( 21 June 2019; 15:54 h) and, also, presents the station’s namesake. This editorial wants to show that the boundary between nature and culture, between art and urban, between human and wild can be much more indefinite than we are accustomed to think. ‘The truth is, we're colorful’, Color Design and Psychology.

Learn more

Everything in our life creates feelings and emotions in us. If it was otherwise, nothing would make sense: each gesture, action, creation, and each experience. Moreover, our sensibility to colours establishes some emotions. The visible reality itself is coloured, in all of its aspects that surround us, and these colours evoke in us emotions and vibrations. The man, seen as both natural being and social being, experiences every day the emotions that the colour evokes with its history, its evolution and its ownerships. This is the starting point addressed that constitute the ‘The Truth Is We Are Coloured’.

The project is about the psychology of colours, more specifically about colours’ symbolism and how we use them in our everyday life. The colour is a daily mystery that belongs to the life of each. For this reason I decided to analyze it and  then translate it into images that evoke such feelings.

This is my thesis project for my degree in New Technologies for Art at the Academy of Fine Arts. It was realize through a deep investigation and intersection between different fields of research such as history, philosophy, art, psychology and design. It was all made possible thank to the guidance of Francesca Valan, an italian Color Designer based in Milan. Take a look at the video and at the thesis’s research. Year: 2017 Art Direction Creative Concept Photography Editing Video Making
