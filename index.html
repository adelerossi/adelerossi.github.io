Introduction

My name is Adele, I am passionate about art and its history, poetry and psychology. My deep love for art has always left a space to be creative myself: I experiment with design, photography, interior decor, video making, fine art, and graphic design too. I love travelling and discovering new cultures, and seeing the world from different perspectives to help build my the future. I began my graphic design career in Italy and, looking for new perspectives, I continued my studies in Spain. Inspired by my family (artists and psychologists), I learned how to unite the aesthetical taste connected with the social and the human field. My projects, my work experiences, my ideas, as well as my very own life style are always a reflection of myself. It's my way of being and working.
